## Sofle Settings
My **Sofle** arrived already flashed. But the default layout seemed inconvenient to me!

1) If you ever feel like reflashing it, you can find the config on the **Aliexpress** page. I recommend checking out my modified config on [soffle](https://github.com/kalachlegends/zmk-sofle.git). As I said, it‚Äôs shaped by a year of use and works great for coders!
2) After choosing the repo, fork it. You can look for tools to edit **keymappings**. Of course, you can do it manually with code, but hopefully you‚Äôre not that crazy üò•. Personally, I used this one ‚Äî https://nickcoutsos.github.io/keymap-editor/.

### Editing a ZMK config in https://nickcoutsos.github.io/keymap-editor/

3) After you log in and select the repo with your ZMK config, you‚Äôll see a canvas where you can remap **keymappings**.
![](Pasted%20image%2020250816045816%201.png)

From there it‚Äôs simple ‚Äî just click on the key you want and choose a **Behavior** (there aren‚Äôt that many).

‚ö†Ô∏è **WARNING!!! To speed up your workflow, I recommend using this option**

![](Pasted%20image%2020250816050126.png)

With it, you can quickly set up the **keymappings** you need.

**Behavior** ‚Äî the main behaviors for convenient configuration:
![](Pasted%20image%2020250822094822.png "behaviors")
1) *kp* (KeyPress) ‚Äî a normal keypress
2) *mo* (Momentary Layer) ‚Äî switch to a layer. (Layers are needed to instantly switch layouts; they‚Äôre somewhat like **SHIFT** or **CTRL**)
3) *mt* (Mod/Tap) ‚Äî lets you assign two actions to the same key. For example, if you just press the letter *k*, it types `k`, but if you hold it, it becomes `A`.
4) *ms..* and so on ‚Äî mouse controls.

## Pros, Cons, and Conclusion

**Pros:**
- Maximum customization;
	- With custom combos, text editing and development speed can increase significantly.
- After training, typing speed should improve by ~30% (personally mine went from *50wpm* ‚Üí *90wpm*+);
- Editing code became ~20% easier;
- Show-off points with friends;
- Potential for automation;

**Cons:**
- Time needed to get used to it;
- Not enough keys;
- Not great for gaming (I tried playing Dota ‚Äî not the same experience);
- Long setup time to fully adapt it to your needs;

**Conclusion:**

- With its custom combos and expandability, I think this can become the best keyboard out there. The only thing I‚Äôd change is the switches ‚Äî I‚Äôd replace them with laptop-style ones.
- **Sofle** is not only a file sharer but also a terminal machine, a Bluetooth connector, and a *2000x accelerator*.
